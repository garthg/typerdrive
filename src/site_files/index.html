Typerdrive: An adaptive computer keyboard instrument

What this is

I am doing a human-computer interaction research study on Mechanical Turk that involved a music creation portion with an instrument I'm developing. I received some positive feedback about it on Reddit, so I wanted to make the fun part of the HIT available to anyone who didn't get a chance to do it on Mechanical Turk. Right now it's just that page from the study, but if I get a chance I'll try to clean it up a bit and make it easier to use. Fair warning: it works best on Chrome, though Firefox and IE9 seem to be pretty okay; older computers may not do well with it.

Play the instrument

The instrument can be configured in different ways. At the moment, there is only one backing track to play along with, but there are a few different ways the keyboard can be layed out.

  • Play with all notes available (chromatic scale)
  • Play with a single scale, chosen specifically for the backing track (pentatonic scale)
  • Play the adaptive version

You can also make your own scale by choosing from the checkboxes below.
